RGB Chameleon

The Scratch game I made for CS50

GOAL

  • Last as long as possible.
  • Avoid projectiles that are of a different color than the the player.
  • Touch projectiles that are of the same color as the player to destroy them.
  • The player's color always matches the background.
  • bounce off corners;
  • change color when they bounce;
  • speed up and target the player when they bounce.

CONTROLS

  • : Move
  • : Switch lanes
  • : Reset
